Fullbody Workouts
If you are a natural bodybuilder, fullbody workouts may be a great option for you. They help provide stimulus to the same muscle groups 3 times per week!
Main exercises
Barbell bench press targets chest, shoulders, and tripces.
Squats target the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.
Deadlifts target the back, hips, legs, and core.
Pull-ups target the back and biceps.
